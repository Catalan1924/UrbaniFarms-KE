About UrbaniFarm KE

Our Purpose

To humanize urban farming, helping families like those in drought-hit areas access affordable, fresh produce via technology that's as easy as checking your phone.

We combat food insecurity, where 70% of urban households worry about nutrition, by turning balconies into bountiful gardens.

Ask About Our Mission

Mission & Vision

Mission: Blend tech with heart to provide hydroponic kits that grow food and family connections, sustainably.

Vision: A nourished Kenya where urban homes thrive as farms, resilient to climate woes.
